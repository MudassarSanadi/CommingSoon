Core Features

Everything You Need to Run Your Dairy Business

🥛

Milk Collection Management

Record morning and evening milk intake from registered farmers. Track quantity, fat %, SNF, and quality parameters per session.

🔬

Automatic Analyzer Integration

Connect directly to Lactoscan, MCC, Milkotronic, and other analyzers. Values are auto-imported — no manual entry errors.

👨‍🌾

Farmer Management

Maintain complete farmer profiles, bank details, cattle information, and history. Easily register new farmers and manage groups.

💰

Payment & Billing

Automatic payment calculation based on fat/SNF rate charts. Generate payment slips, deduction sheets, and advance management.

Advanced Reporting

Daily, weekly, and monthly reports. Milk procurement reports, fat summaries, payment statements, and trend analysis.

Print & Export

Print receipts, farmer slips, and reports. Export data to Excel or PDF for accounting and compliance purposes.

Inventory Management

Track dairy products inventory, packaging materials, and supplies with stock alerts and reorder management.

Admin, supervisor, and operator roles with configurable permissions. Complete audit trail for all transactions.
